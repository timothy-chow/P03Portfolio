Reflection

Still in the midst of working at The Peak for my second term, I've enjoyed and learned quite a bit in working and collaborating with my fellow editors and production team. I was fortunate enough to be in this position where I could put the idea of "designing for others and not ourselves" into practice in a real context. Early on, I struggled with this concept but I soon learned to frame the layout design with the content itself in order to fully bring the piece together.
